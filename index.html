HOW TO PLAY???

  • 1. Pick a category from the four options
  • 2. You'll receive a randomly assigned word, represented by underscores for each letter
  • 3. Start guessing by clicking letters on the keyboard
  • 4. Each letter click counts as one guess, and you can click the same letter multiple times without penalty
  • 5. Be cautious; you have a limit of 5 incorrect guesses, a wrong guess reduces your remaining attempts
  • 6. Continue guessing until you either complete the word or exhaust your allowed guesses
  • P.S Winning or losing brings its own surprises!
